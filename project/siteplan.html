Website Plan

1. Site Name

Healthy Living Hub

This name was selected because it represents a central place for resources and information related to healthy living, encompassing various aspects such as nutrition, fitness, mental well-being, and more. It is easy to remember and clearly communicates the purpose of the site.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of the Healthy Living Hub website is to provide comprehensive resources and information to individuals seeking to improve their overall health and well-being. The site will offer articles, guides, and tools related to nutrition, exercise, mental health, and lifestyle tips. Additionally, it will feature a community forum where users can share experiences and advice.

3. Scenarios

  • What are some effective meal plans for weight loss?
  • Where can I find support and advice for managing stress?
  • What can I do to be mentally and emotionally fit?

4. Color Schema

Primary Color: #2c3e50 (Dark Blue) - used for headings and the navigation bar.

Secondary Color: #e74c3c (Red) - used for accents, buttons, and links.

5. Typography

Primary Font: Arial, sans-serif - used for body text and general content.

Secondary Font: Georgia, serif - used for headings and subheadings to add a classic and elegant touch.
